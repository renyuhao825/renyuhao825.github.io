Biography
I'm a PhD student of Department of Civil and Enviromental Engineering in UVA, supervised by Zhen (Leo) Liu. Previously, I finished my Masters degree at HNU CEE, advised by Prof. Chao Zhang. Before that, I received my undergraduate degree from Chongqing Jiaotong University. I am broadly interested in scientific machine learning and computational mechanics, especially in solving inverse problems in poromechanics with physics-informed learning techniques.
